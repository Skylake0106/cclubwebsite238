GVSU Computing Club

Companies

The Computing Club is fortunate to have associated with multiple locally renowned businesses that specialize in or utilize computing-based systems. Here are a few of the recent highlights and upcoming events where we've gotten the opportunity to learn more about these companies.

Open Systems Technologies

Open Systems Technologies (OST) is a business technology firm that helps their clientele excel with strategy & insight concentrated on connected products. Their main services include data center transformation & enterprise managed services (including but not limited to; custom & cross platform app development, data analytics, and cloud solutions).

On March 29th, the Computing Club took a trip to OST in Grand Rapids for a tour from 4 employees, 3 of which being GVSU Alumni. They detailed the general company operations and personal day-to-day processes, the application/hiring process, the necessary & developed skills, and plenty more.

Herman Miller

Herman Miller (soon MillerKnoll) is a huge name in Furniture & Home Furnishings Manufacturing, globally recognized for their innovative designs. They're best known for partnering with independent designers to design solutions for specific issues a person or company is undergoing.

On April 14th, they're bringing over a panel of employees to have a talk with the Computing Club to go over who is on the panel, ask them a series of questions about their professional goals, their work life at Herman Miller, what projects they work on, technology they use, and more.

Atomic Objects

Atomic Object is a Software Development company that creates applications (for web, mobile, desktop, and device) specializing in custom product design and development, atomic embedded development, software consulting, and more.

On April 7th, Atomic Objects plans to send an employee to GVSU for a presentation discussing life at Atomic Object from the daily routine to specific technologies and projects they're involved with.
