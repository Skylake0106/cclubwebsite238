Winter 2022 Schedule

Meeting 3/31 : Graduate School Presentation

The Graduate School will be coming in to talk to us about graduate college and who it might be a right fit for, answer questions about getting a masters, and answer some more specific computing masters questions as the head of the Applied Computing Masters Program will be attending.

Meeting 4/7 : Atomic Object Presentation

Atomic Object will be sending an employee over to talk to us about Atomic Object and what they work on there, what technologies they use, and how life at Atomic Object is.

Meeting 4/14 : Herman Miller (MillerKnoll) Presentation

Herman Miller will be bringing over a panel of employees to have a talk with the Computing Club. We will go over who is on the panel, and ask them a series of questions about their professional goals, their work life at Herman Miller, what projects they work on and technology they use, and more.

Meeting 4/21 : Study Night (For Finals)

This meeting we will be just having a relaxing study session for finals, where we can all come to the clubroom and help each other study on parts we are stuck on or just provide a space to be productive in.

Meeting 4/28 : No Meeting (Finals Week)

Due to Finals week taking place no meeting will be held have a great summer! Hope to see you back in the fall for another semester of Computing Club!
